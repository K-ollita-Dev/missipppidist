Classes

 Programa Fulbright-Hays

Students embark on excursions to important Bolivian locations including the Salar de Uyuni, Madidi National Park, Potosí, and Lake Titicaca, blending tourism with academic study.

Students visit cool places in Bolivia like the Salar de Uyuni, Madidi National Park, Potosí, and Lake Titicaca, combining fun and learning.

In workshops led by expert faculty, students tackle topics such as health, politics, and community-based research. These workshops allow students to apply their theoretical knowledge to real-world situations, contributing to a deeper understanding of Bolivian reality.

Students get hands-on experience in workshops led by experts on topics like health, politics, and community research, which helps them understand Bolivia better.

Students will participate in a cultural immersion program, living with carefully selected Bolivian host families. This daily interaction provides firsthand experience of Bolivian customs and traditions.

The Waliki Friends student volunteer network creates spaces for integration between international students and the local community through in-person recreational and cultural activities.
